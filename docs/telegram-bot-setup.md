# Get Your Telegram Bot Token

This takes 2 minutes. You need the Telegram app on your phone or desktop.

---

## Step 1 — Open BotFather

BotFather is Telegram's official bot creator. It's verified by Telegram.

1. Open Telegram
2. In the search bar, type **@BotFather**
3. Tap the result with the blue checkmark ✓
4. Tap **Start**

---

## Step 2 — Create your bot

Type this command and send it:

```
/newbot
```

BotFather will ask two questions:

**Question 1: What is the name of your bot?**

This is the display name people see in chats. Choose something that represents you or your service. Examples:

- `Debbie Marketing`
- `OO Agency Assistant`
- `My SEO Bot`

Type your chosen name and send it.

**Question 2: What username should I give it?**

This is the @handle — must be unique and must end in `bot`. Examples:

- `debbie_marketing_bot`
- `oo_agency_bot`
- `myseo_bot`

Type your chosen username and send it. If it's taken, BotFather will ask you to try another.

---

## Step 3 — Copy your token

When your bot is created, BotFather sends a message like this:

```
Done! Congratulations on your new bot. You will find it at t.me/your_bot_name

Use this token to access the HTTP API:
1234567890:AAExxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

Keep your token secure and store it safely, it can be used by anyone to control your bot.
```

**Copy the token** — it's the long string that looks like `1234567890:AAExxxxxxx...`

---

## Step 4 — Send it to Anthony

Send the token to Anthony on Telegram: **@toc**

Just paste it as a message. He'll wire it up to your agent and your bot will be live within minutes.

---

## That's it

Once Anthony has the token, your bot will:
- Respond to anyone who messages it on Telegram
- Route briefs to your 11 specialist agents
- Reply in ~3 seconds

`https://t.me/your_bot_username`
