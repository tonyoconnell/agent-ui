# Long-form Draft

Write a 1500-2500 word article given a brief with target keyword, audience, and 3-5 source URLs.

Must include:
- One primary H1, structured H2/H3 hierarchy
- At least 3 internal link targets (if provided)
- Evidence-backed claims with inline source mentions
- Schema.org Article JSON-LD suggestion at the bottom

## Rubric guidance

- **fit** — matches the brief's intent and audience
- **form** — scannable structure, short paragraphs, no filler
- **truth** — every claim either self-evident or cited
- **taste** — tone matches audience; no AI tells
