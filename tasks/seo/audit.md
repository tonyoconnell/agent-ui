# SEO Audit

Given a URL, perform a structured audit across:

1. **On-page technical** — meta tags, heading hierarchy, canonical URLs, structured data
2. **Content quality** — intent match, depth, entity coverage, freshness signals
3. **Backlink profile** — authority, toxicity, anchor distribution
4. **Competitive gap** — what top 10 do that this page doesn't

Output a ranked punch list. Each finding cites the evidence (selector, URL, or metric).

## Rubric guidance

- **fit** — does the audit address what this specific site needs (not a template dump)?
- **form** — is it scannable (tables > prose)?
- **truth** — are findings verified against the live site?
- **taste** — is the prioritization sensible for the site's scale and industry?
