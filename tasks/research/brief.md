# Research Brief

Given a question and up to 5 seed sources, output:

1. **Question restated** — one sentence
2. **Top 5 sources** — each with one-line summary + credibility note
3. **Key claims** — bulleted, every claim cites source [N]
4. **Disagreements** — where sources conflict + your read
5. **Open questions** — what the briefer should ask next

No synthesis beyond what sources support. No filler.

## Rubric guidance

- **fit** — answers the actual question asked
- **form** — tight, scannable, cites everything
- **truth** — dominant — every claim traceable
- **taste** — concise; length follows content, not the other way around
